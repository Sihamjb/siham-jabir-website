About Me

Hi, I'm Siham Jabir, but you can call me Sahouma. I'm an artist who loves to paint and create digital art. As a freelance graphic designer, I enjoy working on projects that involve Arabic calligraphy and creating print-on-demand (POD) designs. I hold a BA in English Literature and a diploma in Graphic Design.
